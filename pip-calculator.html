What is a Pip?

A pip (percentage in point) is the smallest price move that a given exchange rate can make. For most currency pairs, a pip is 0.0001, except for pairs involving the Japanese Yen where it's 0.01.

Understanding pip value is crucial for risk management and position sizing in forex trading. This calculator helps you determine the monetary value of each pip movement in your trades.
